# SchoolPurse — School Setup Guide

A step-by-step walkthrough for getting your school fully running after you sign
in for the first time. Do the steps in order — each one builds on the last.
Most schools finish in 20–30 minutes.

> **Who does this?** The first person to sign up is the **Head / Admin**. Only
> the Admin can change settings and invite team members. Bursars and teachers
> are added later (Step 7).

---

## Before you start
- You'll sign in with the **same email you paid with** — no password, just a
  secure link sent to your inbox ("magic link").
- Have ready: your school's name, logo, fee structure (what you charge per
  term), and your class/grade list.

---

## Step 1 — Finish onboarding
After clicking the sign-in link, you'll land on the **"Set up your school"**
screen. Enter:
1. **School name** (appears on receipts & invoices)
2. *(Optional)* URL slug, receipt prefix (auto-filled — fine to keep),
   currency, terms per year, address, phone
3. **Your name** (printed on receipts)

Click **Create my school**. You're now in your dashboard.

---

## Step 2 — School information & branding
Go to **Settings** (left sidebar).

- **Branding** → upload your **school logo**. It appears on the sidebar,
  receipts, and invoices.
- **School information** → confirm name, address, phone, **currency**,
  **receipt prefix** (e.g. `TSJS` → receipts read `TSJS-2026-000001`), and
  **terms per year** (usually 3 in Zimbabwe).

Save each section.

---

## Step 3 — Turn on your school levels  ⭐ (this creates your classes)
Still in **Settings → School levels**.

Toggle on the sections your school runs (e.g. **Primary**, **Secondary**).
**Turning a level on automatically creates its standard class list** (ECD A/B,
Grade 1–7, Forms, etc.) — so you don't have to add classes one by one.

---

## Step 4 — Review & adjust classes
**Settings → Classes.**

Check the auto-created classes. Rename, remove, or add any that don't match your
school. (Bursars take payments against a student's class, so get this right.)

---

## Step 5 — Set up your fee items
**Settings → Fee items.** Add everything you charge, for example:
- **Tuition** (per term)
- **Levy / development fund** (per term)
- **Sports, ICT, exam, transport, boarding** (as needed)

For each fee item set:
- **Amount** (in your currency)
- **Recurrence**: per term / per month / one-off
- **Applicable classes** (e.g. tuition differs by grade)
- **Include on registration** (charged when a new student is enrolled)

Then **Settings → Uniforms** for any uniform items you sell.

---

## Step 6 — Set your current academic term  ⚠️ important
Invoices and the "fee collection rate" need a **current term**.

> **Heads up:** there isn't a self-service screen for academic year/term yet.
> Your first year + term are set up **with you during onboarding** — if your
> Settings page shows *"No current term is set,"* contact SchoolPurse support
> and we'll set it in minutes. (Coming soon: create terms yourself.)

Once a term is active, **Settings → Term operations** shows it.

---

## Step 7 — Invite your team
**Settings → Team → Invite.** Each teammate gets an email invite and signs in
the same way. Choose a role — **roles control exactly what each person sees:**

| Role | Can see / do |
|------|--------------|
| **Head / Admin** | Everything — settings, team, fees, finance, students, reports |
| **Bursar** | Payments, arrears, students, expenses, reports & P&L. **Cannot** open Settings or manage the team. |
| **Teacher** | Overview + Students only. **No** finance, payments, or settings. |

Pick the least access someone needs — you can change roles anytime.

---

## Step 8 — Add your students
**Students** (left sidebar).

- **Add student** one at a time, **or**
- **Import CSV** for the whole school at once (fastest). Assign each student to
  a class. Enrolling a student bills any "include on registration" fees.

---

## Step 9 — Generate term invoices
**Settings → Term operations → Generate invoices.**

This creates this term's invoices for **every active student**, based on the
per-term fee items that apply to their class. Students who already have an
invoice for the term are skipped, so it's safe to re-run.

---

## Step 10 — Record payments & issue receipts
**Payments** (or the **Quick Payment** button up top).

- Select the student, enter the amount and method (cash, bank transfer, mobile
  money), and save.
- A **receipt** is generated — **print** or **email** it to the parent.
- Recorded payments reduce the student's balance automatically.

---

## Step 11 — Track arrears & watch your numbers
- **Arrears** — every student with an outstanding balance, largest first.
- **Overview** — today's collections, monthly income, outstanding, net.
- **Reports & P&L** — income vs expenses over time, fee collection rate.
- **Expenses** — log running costs so your P&L is accurate.

---

## Quick setup checklist
- [ ] Logo + school info saved
- [ ] School levels turned on (classes created)
- [ ] Classes reviewed
- [ ] Fee items added
- [ ] Current term set
- [ ] Team invited (right roles)
- [ ] Students added / imported
- [ ] Term invoices generated
- [ ] First payment recorded + receipt issued

Once these are ticked, your school is fully live on SchoolPurse. 🎉
