# SchoolPurse — Competitor Intelligence

*Single source of truth for comparison pages. Verify pricing quarterly. Be fair: acknowledge real strengths — disparagement loses evaluators.*

## How to read this
SchoolPurse is a **focused fee tool**, not a full school ERP. Most "competitors" are either (a) the manual default, or (b) all-in-one ERPs that also do fees. Our wedge is focus + local fit + price + speed.

---

## 1. Spreadsheets + paper cash book  *(the real #1 alternative)*
- **What it is:** Excel/Google Sheets and a carbon receipt book.
- **Strengths:** free, familiar, flexible, no vendor.
- **Weaknesses:** receipts not numbered/traceable; totals drift; arrears invisible until term-end (~3 weeks to compile a report); breaks when two staff edit; no roles or audit trail.
- **Best for:** very small schools with a handful of students.
- **How SchoolPurse wins:** real-time arrears, numbered receipts, no drift, roles + audit — for less than the fees lost in one untracked term.

## 2. Sekani  *(Zimbabwe — closest direct competitor)*
- **Positioning:** Zimbabwe-built school management software. Invoices, real-time payment tracking, USD/ZWL, QR-code student IDs, web-based.
- **Strengths:** local (built for ZW), QR student IDs / access control, real-time tracking, multi-currency.
- **Weaknesses (assess directly):** broader/heavier than a pure fee tool; pricing on request; mobile-first / low-end Android experience to be verified.
- **Best for:** ZW schools wanting management + ID/access features in one.
- **How SchoolPurse wins:** narrower and faster on the fee job specifically, mobile-first, transparent low entry price, sub-30-min setup.

## 3. Smart School Manager / Genius Education ERP  *(Zimbabwe pages, global vendors)*
- **Positioning:** full web-based ERP — academics, LMS, exams, HR, payroll, invoices, expenses, payment gateways.
- **Strengths:** all-in-one; many modules; parent/teacher/student panels.
- **Weaknesses:** heavy to roll out and run; generic/global (localized via a country page, not built for ZW); ERP-tier cost and complexity for a school that mainly needs fees.
- **Best for:** larger schools wanting one system for everything.
- **How SchoolPurse wins:** focused, affordable, fast; no ERP rollout to win fees.

## 4. Fedena  *(global / pan-African ERP)*
- **Positioning:** all-in-one school ERP, 50+ modules including online fees.
- **Pricing:** publicly cited from ~$1,000+/yr; custom plans.
- **Strengths:** mature, feature-rich, established.
- **Weaknesses:** reported to need customization for local (African) payment rails; cost and complexity high for budget-conscious schools.
- **Best for:** well-resourced schools wanting a full ERP.
- **How SchoolPurse wins:** local payments out of the box, far lower entry price, minutes-not-months to value.

## 5. Edves  *(Nigeria / pan-African ERP)*
- **Positioning:** school management + LMS + finance suite, strong in Nigeria, expanding across Africa.
- **Strengths:** comprehensive, pan-African brand, local payment integrations.
- **Weaknesses:** full-suite scope = more to adopt than a fee-only need; ZW-specific fit (EcoCash, USD, terms) to be verified.
- **Best for:** schools wanting an African all-in-one platform.
- **How SchoolPurse wins:** ZW-native fit and a focused, cheaper fee product.

## 6. Zeraki  *(Kenya — analytics + management)*
- **Positioning:** school analytics + management, strong in Kenya; M-Pesa-centric.
- **Strengths:** analytics depth, established in East Africa.
- **Weaknesses:** Kenya/M-Pesa-centric; not tuned for ZW (USD/EcoCash/terms).
- **Best for:** Kenyan schools wanting analytics + management.
- **How SchoolPurse wins:** Southern-Africa / ZW fit and fee focus.

*(Adjacent: Elimikasasa (Kenya) — free under 100 students, M-Pesa; CBZ school-fees payment channel — a bank rail, not management software.)*
