# SchoolPurse — Campaign Positioning

**Goal:** school demos & signups · **Geo:** Zimbabwe first · **Channel mix:** WhatsApp, Instagram, email, Facebook, paid social.

## One-line positioning
**SchoolPurse is the fee book your school should already have** — it shows you exactly who has paid, who owes, and prints a receipt the second you record a payment. Parents keep paying by cash, bank or EcoCash.

## Who we are talking to
- **Primary: the Bursar / Accounts clerk** — does the daily grind of receipting cash and reconciling the book. Feels the pain most.
- **Decision-maker: the Head / Director** — signs off the spend, cares about leakage, arrears, and looking professional to parents.
- **Context:** private, independent, mission and trust primary & secondary schools in Zimbabwe. 80–1,000 students. WhatsApp-first. Pays in USD.

## The problem (their words, not ours)
- "I only find out who hasn't paid at the end of term."
- "The receipt book and the bank don't agree."
- "We lose money and we can't even prove it."
- "Reconciling fees takes the bursar days."

## The outcome we sell
Know your money in real time. Every payment receipted instantly. Every arrear visible the day it happens. Hours of reconciliation gone — without changing how a single parent pays.

## Positioning statement
For Zimbabwean schools drowning in cash receipt books and untracked arrears, SchoolPurse is fee-tracking software that records every payment, prints an instant numbered receipt, and shows live arrears and collection rate — unlike spreadsheets or a paper book, it never drifts and never hides who owes.

## Three messaging pillars
1. **See your money live.** Collections, arrears and collection rate, updated the moment you record a payment.
2. **Receipts in seconds.** Numbered, branded, print or send on WhatsApp — no more torn-out stubs.
3. **Built for here.** USD, EcoCash/OneMoney/InnBucks, Zim terms, runs on any Android phone. Made in Harare.

## Proof points
- Set up in under 30 minutes (levels auto-create your class list).
- Role-based: bursar sees finance, teacher sees only students.
- Each school's data isolated (row-level security).
- From 29 USD/month (≤200 students); 79 USD/month (≤1000).

## Top objections → reframes
- "Parents won't use an app." → They don't touch it. It's internal — only your staff log in. Parents pay exactly as they do today.
- "We already use a spreadsheet." → Spreadsheets drift, don't number receipts, and break when two people edit them. This doesn't.
- "We have no budget." → It costs less than the fees you lose to one untracked term. Start on a demo, free.
- "Switching is a hassle." → Import your students by CSV, toggle your levels, done in 30 minutes. We help you do it.

## Tone profile
Practical, plain-spoken, locally credible, founder-led. Speak like a fellow Zimbabwean who built a useful thing — not a SaaS brochure. Avoid: "revolutionize", "seamless", "cutting-edge", emojis-as-bullets, corporate hedging.
