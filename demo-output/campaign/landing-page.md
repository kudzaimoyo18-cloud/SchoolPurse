## Problem
### Right now, your fees live in a book.
Cash comes in, someone writes a stub, and at the end of term the receipt book and the bank never quite agree. Arrears stay invisible until it's too late to chase them. The school is busiest exactly when its money is hardest to see — and that's where money leaks.

## How it works
1. **Set your fee items** — tuition, levy, sports, exams. Map them to classes.
2. **Invoice the term** — every student billed the right amount, in one batch.
3. **Record payments** — cash, bank, or mobile money. A numbered receipt prints instantly.
4. **See arrears live** — who owes, largest first, plus your collection rate — any day, not just at term-end.

## Features (benefit-led)
- **Live dashboard** — today's collections, monthly income, outstanding, net. At a glance.
- **Instant receipts** — numbered (e.g. TSJS-2026-000412), branded, print or send on WhatsApp.
- **Arrears that chase themselves** — a ranked list of who owes what, updated in real time.
- **Reports & P&L** — income vs expenses, fee-collection rate, term by term.
- **Roles done right** — the bursar sees finance; teachers see only students; the head sees everything.
- **Your data, isolated** — row-level security so no school ever sees another's numbers.
