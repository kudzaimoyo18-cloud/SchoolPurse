# SchoolPurse — Demand & Acquisition Plan (Zimbabwe, IG-led launch)

> Framework adapted from the Series-A demand playbook to a low-budget, mobile-first, single-market reality. ICP is institutional (schools) but reached through individual decision-makers on consumer channels (Instagram, Facebook, WhatsApp).

## ICP & buying unit
- **Economic buyer:** Head teacher / School Head, Board Treasurer.
- **Champion / daily user:** Bursar, school accounts clerk.
- **Influencers:** SDC/SDA committee, deputy head.
- A school is one "account"; expect 1–3 humans involved. Decision is relationship-led, not self-serve — content builds trust, a conversation closes.

## Funnel (mapped to channels you actually have)
| Stage | Goal | Channels | Asset |
|-------|------|----------|-------|
| TOFU | Awareness among school staff | IG feed + Reels, FB page, FB groups (Zim teachers/heads), WhatsApp status | The 5 launch posts, receipts reel |
| MOFU | Interest → conversation | IG DM, WhatsApp Business, link-in-bio, demo video | Demo video, "book a walkthrough" |
| BOFU | Walkthrough → first school live | 1:1 WhatsApp/call demo, onboarding wizard, roster import | Onboarding, first-payment recorded |

## Channel priority (ranked for this market)
1. **WhatsApp Business** — the real CRM here. Catalogue + quick replies + broadcast lists. Every IG/FB CTA routes to WhatsApp, not a web form (forms die on patchy data).
2. **Instagram** — credibility + reach. Reels for distribution, feed for proof. Link-in-bio → WhatsApp.
3. **Facebook Page + Groups** — older head-teacher demographic lives on FB. Join/post in Zim education + bursar groups (value first, no spam).
4. **Direct outreach** — list schools by district, walk in / call the bursar. Highest close rate; content warms them first.
5. **Referral loop** — every live school refers 2 peer schools (heads talk to heads).

## Low-budget paid (start $5–10/day, Meta only)
- **Objective:** Engagement/Traffic → WhatsApp (Click-to-WhatsApp ads convert best here).
- **Audience:** Geo Zimbabwe (Harare, Bulawayo first); interests: teachers, school administration, education; age 30–60.
- **Creative:** Boost Post 4 (receipts reel) + Post 7 (built-for-Zim). Test 2 creatives, keep the cheaper cost-per-WhatsApp-reply.
- **Scale rule:** if cost per qualified WhatsApp chat < ~$3, raise budget 20%/week. Cut losers weekly.

## Lead capture & qualification
- Capture in WhatsApp: school name, district, # students, role, current method (paper/Excel).
- **SQL = "Qualified school":** decision-maker + has a roster + wants a walkthrough in ≤2 weeks.
- Track in a simple sheet (Name · District · Students · Stage · Next action) until volume justifies a CRM.

## Growth loops
- **Referral:** after a school's first term-report, ask the head to intro 2 peer heads → offer both a free setup session.
- **Receipt-as-billboard:** every WhatsApp/printed receipt carries the SchoolPurse mark + handle → parents/other schools see it.
- **Proof loop:** turn each happy bursar quote into a new post (like Post 3) → more trust → more chats.

## Risks / guardrails
- **Single-channel risk:** don't depend only on IG — FB + direct + referral in parallel.
- **Form friction:** never force a web form on a school over mobile data; WhatsApp first.
- **Trust:** financial product — lead every touch with isolation/roles/audit (Post 6) and real bursar quotes.
- **Don't buy followers / engagement** — vanity metrics, zero pipeline.
